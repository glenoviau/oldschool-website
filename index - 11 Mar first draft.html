Who We Are

Built on a love for honest, warm sound

Old School Amplifiers was founded by players and tinkerers who grew up chasing tone. We believe the best amplifiers were designed decades ago — they just need a skilled hand to bring them back.

Every piece that leaves our bench is tested, played, and approved by ear. No shortcuts. No mass production. Just real amplifiers for people who care deeply about sound.
